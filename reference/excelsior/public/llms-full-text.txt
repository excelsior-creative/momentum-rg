# Excelsior Creative - Full Site Summary for AI Training

## About
Excelsior Creative is a trusted technology partner for national nonprofits, specializing in AI-powered solutions, custom software engineering, and digital strategy to amplify world-changing missions. Founded in 2015, we are based in Irvine, California, and serve clients across the United States.

## Mission
We help nonprofits and mission-driven organizations leverage cutting-edge technology to maximize their impact. Our team combines deep expertise in nonprofit operations with advanced technical capabilities in AI, automation, and modern web development.

## Core Services

### Web Development
Professional WordPress and Elementor development for businesses that need impact and speed. For complex, performance-critical applications, we build custom React and Next.js sites with AI-driven features and sub-second load times. Includes WordPress & Elementor development, React & Next.js custom applications, AI-enhanced development, performance optimization, technical SEO, and headless CMS integration.

### Agentic Solutions
AI-powered autonomous agents and intelligent automation systems that handle complex workflows, data processing, and decision-making. Includes custom AI agent development, workflow automation, LLM integration, intelligent data processing, and autonomous system architecture.

### Software Development
Full-stack product development from MVP to scale-ready platforms. We architect and build entire technical foundations including React, Next.js, mobile apps, APIs, and databases. Includes full-stack development, mobile application development, API design and development, database architecture, and cloud infrastructure.

### Brand Development
Comprehensive brand strategy and identity design. Logo design, visual identity systems, messaging frameworks, and brand guidelines that position organizations as market leaders. Includes brand strategy, visual identity design, messaging frameworks, brand guidelines, and go-to-market strategy.

### Managed Web Hosting
Reliable, secure, and optimized hosting solutions with 24/7 monitoring and support. Includes managed WordPress hosting, cloud infrastructure management, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support.

### Launchpad
Complete startup launch services combining product development, brand strategy, and go-to-market execution. Includes full-stack product development, brand strategy and identity, AI and automation integration, go-to-market strategy, startup advisory and mentorship, and equity partnership models.
